A curated, community-driven collection of interview questions (with model answers, frameworks, and explanations) for **Genomics Data Scientist / Computational Biologist / Bioinformatics Scientist** roles — spanning academic labs, biotech/pharma, clinical genomics companies, and sequencing/platform companies.

This is not a list of trivia. Every question includes:
- **Why interviewers ask it**
- **A model answer or framework**
- **Follow-up questions** interviewers commonly use to probe deeper

> 🌱 This is v1. Contributions, corrections, and new questions are very welcome — see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).

> ⚠️ **Note on scope:** This repo focuses on the computational/statistical side of genomics (data types, pipelines, statistical methods, ML applications). It assumes some existing background in either biology or data science/statistics (most candidates come from one side and are building fluency in the other) — it is not a substitute for a molecular biology or statistics course.

## 🧭 How to Use This Repo

- **Coming from a pure data science/statistics/CS background?** Prioritize sections 1 and 2 first — you'll need working fluency in molecular biology vocabulary and genomic file formats before your statistical skills are useful in this domain.
- **Coming from a wet-lab molecular biology/genetics background moving into a computational role?** Prioritize sections 3, 4, and 6 — the goal is building rigor in statistical methods, pipeline mechanics, and reproducibility practices.
- **Interviewing at a clinical genomics or diagnostics company?** Focus heavily on sections 4 and 7 — variant interpretation rigor and regulatory/privacy awareness are especially weighted here.
- **Interviewing for a single-cell or spatial genomics-focused role?** Focus heavily on section 5.
- **Interviewing at an academic-adjacent research group or core facility?** Focus on sections 3, 4, and 6 — deep methodological and reproducibility rigor tends to matter most.

Each question is tagged with a rough difficulty and role-level indicator:
- 🟢 Junior/Associate · 🟡 Mid-level · 🔴 Senior/Principal
